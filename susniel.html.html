About Me

Born to Vietnamese parents, I was immersed in a family rich with stories of heritage, traditional values, and resilience. The name "Monsoon" represents the mighty force of nature that runs through my bloodline—dynamic, unpredictable, and transformative. My middle name "King" reflects my commitment to leadership, striving to guide others while fostering unity in diversity.

As a cultural ambassador, my mission is to blend the ancient with the contemporary, forging new pathways for cultural dialogues through art, music, and entrepreneurship. Drawing inspiration from my Vietnamese roots, I aim to bring traditions into the global spotlight while preserving their integrity and beauty.

Projects

The Monsoon Art Collective

This collective gathers Southeast Asian artists to create interdisciplinary art that bridges cultures, addressing the diaspora experience and the global exchange of creative energy.

King's Leadership Academy

A global leadership initiative that empowers young people from diverse backgrounds to understand and implement principles of ethical leadership, social justice, and sustainable growth.

Cultural Renaissance Festival

Celebrating the beauty of Vietnamese and Asian cultures through a dynamic fusion of traditional performances, fashion, food, and modern artistic expression.
